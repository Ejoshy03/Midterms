WELCOME TO Surigao City

About us

Surigao City, situated on the northeastern coast of Mindanao Island in the Philippines, is a captivating destination known for its stunning natural wonders and rich cultural heritage. Surigao boasts numerous breathtaking islands and beaches with pristine white sands and crystal-clear waters. The city is a treasure trove of stunning natural wonders, each more captivating than the last. From majestic limestone cliffs to lush verdant landscapes, Surigao's diverse terrain is a playground for adventurers and nature enthusiasts alike. The coastline is adorned with numerous islands and beaches, boasting powdery white sands that gently meet the crystal-clear waters of the Pacific Ocean. Here, one can bask in the serenity of secluded coves or embark on exhilarating water sports adventures, such as snorkeling, diving, and surfing.

.

Hagonoy Island

Hagonoy Island boasts breathtaking natural beauty, featuring powdery white sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and lush greenery. Visitors are greeted with panoramic views of the surrounding seascape, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and recreation.

Mabua Pebble Beach

Mabua Pebble Beach is renowned for its unique landscape characterized by an expansive shoreline covered in smooth, rounded pebbles instead of sand. These colorful pebbles, ranging in size from tiny stones to larger rocks, create a mesmerizing mosaic of colors and textures along the coast.

Sohoton

Sohoton Cove is perfectly covered with valleys, hilltops, soft-sandy white beaches, beautiful forests and mountains. The best way to explore and discover every nook and corner of the enigmatic and charming island is on a pump boat.

Magpupungko Beach

Magpupunko beach is a hidden gem renowned for its mesmerizing natural beauty and unique tidal pools. Tucked away from the bustling city life, Magpupungko offers a serene escape into a world of pristine white sands and azure waters, inviting travelers to unwind and immerse themselves in the splendor of nature.
